About Mardiya

I am Mardiya, a mother with a deep love
for meaningful words and beautiful design.

To me, a poem is never just words.
It is emotion gently placed into sentences,
a memory preserved with care,
a feeling expressed in a way the heart understands.

Each poem I write is personal and sincere,
created for moments of love, joy,
remembrance and connection.

Alongside this, I offer beautifully printed
and framed pieces, so your words
can be held, displayed and treasured
for years to come.

Poems written with care, sincerity
and from the heart.
