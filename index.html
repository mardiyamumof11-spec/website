A Little About Me

About Mardiya

I’m Mardiya, a mother of eleven with a deep love for meaningful words and beautiful design.

To me, a card is never just an image. It is emotion wrapped in soft colours. It is a memory preserved in thoughtful detail. It is words chosen with care so they feel as beautiful as they look.

I create personalised digital cards and write each poem myself, whether for joy, celebration, gratitude, remembrance, friendship, marriage, Eid, or simply because someone deserves to feel seen.

For my customers in the UK, I also offer beautifully printed physical cards, canvas pieces and framed designs so your message can be held, displayed and treasured for years to come.
